# MetaPersona Homepage Wireframe (v0.8.1 Visual Design)

## Visual Design Elements
- Background: Light grey (soft, noticeable)
- Header: Thin white bar, radiant glow (purple→gold→pink→blue) at bottom edge
- Header Content:
    - Top left: [Login] [Signup] (black, white on hover)
    - Below left: [Language Selector]
    - Top right: MetaPersona (black, Copilot-style font)
- Homepage Body: Centered rounded white panel, radiant glow around edges
